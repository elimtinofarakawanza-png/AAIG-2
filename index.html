About the AAIG Group

The Air Africa International Group (AAIG) is a multinational airline alliance created to unify air travel across Africa and connect the continent to the world. AAIG brings together airlines from Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and North America under one shared mission: to improve global connectivity, strengthen African aviation, and deliver a consistent, high‑quality travel experience across all member airlines.

AAIG focuses on cooperation, shared growth, and operational excellence. Member airlines benefit from coordinated schedules, shared routes, expanded networks, and unified service standards. From low‑cost regional flights to premium long‑haul journeys, AAIG represents a new era of aviation led by African leadership and global partnership.

Air Zimbabwe

Air Zimbabwe is the national carrier of Zimbabwe and one of the founding pillars of AAIG. With decades of aviation heritage, it connects Harare to major African and international destinations. Known for its resilience and cultural pride, Air Zimbabwe brings stability, tradition, and national identity to the alliance. Its mission is to rebuild regional strength, expand long‑haul capability, and restore Zimbabwe’s aviation legacy on the global stage.

AerCYMRU

AerCYMRU is the flagship airline of Wales and the lead airline of AAIG. It combines modern European aviation standards with strong Welsh identity, offering a premium yet accessible travel experience. AerCYMRU is known for its ambitious long‑haul routes, rapid expansion, and high‑quality service. As the alliance leader, it sets the tone for innovation, safety, and operational excellence across all AAIG member airlines.

Fastjet MAX

Fastjet MAX is AAIG’s dedicated low‑cost carrier, designed to make air travel affordable across Africa. It focuses on high‑efficiency operations, quick turnarounds, and point‑to‑point connectivity between underserved cities. Despite being low‑cost, Fastjet MAX maintains strong safety standards and reliable service. Its mission is simple: connect Africa’s people, economies, and opportunities.

Arabic Airways

Arabic Airways bridges the Middle East and Africa within AAIG. It offers premium service, modern aircraft, and strong regional connectivity through major hubs in the Gulf region. Known for its hospitality and luxury‑focused cabins, Arabic Airways strengthens AAIG’s global reach and brings a high‑quality service standard to the alliance. It connects Africa to Asia, Europe, and the Middle East with seamless travel experiences.

FlyAmerican

FlyAmerican is AAIG’s North American partner airline, expanding the alliance’s footprint across the United States, Canada, and beyond. It provides seamless transatlantic connections, strong domestic coverage, and access to major U.S. hubs. FlyAmerican brings American aviation scale, reliability, and network strength to AAIG, making global travel smoother for passengers across all continents.
