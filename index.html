About the AAIG Group

The Air Africa International Group (AAIG) is a global airline alliance built to unite continents through powerful aviation partnerships. AAIG blends African leadership with international reach, creating a vibrant network that connects cultures, economies, and people.

With member airlines across Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and North America, AAIG focuses on shared growth, modern aviation standards, and seamless travel experiences. From low‑cost regional flights to premium long‑haul journeys, AAIG represents a new era of colourful, connected aviation.

Air Zimbabwe

Air Zimbabwe stands as a symbol of national pride and African aviation heritage. As a founding member of AAIG, it connects Harare to key African and global destinations. Known for resilience, culture, and identity, Air Zimbabwe brings tradition and strength to the alliance while working to expand long‑haul operations and restore Zimbabwe’s global aviation presence.

AerCYMRU

AerCYMRU is the flagship airline of Wales and the lead airline of AAIG. It blends modern European aviation with Welsh culture, offering premium service and ambitious long‑haul expansion. As the alliance leader, AerCYMRU sets the standard for safety, innovation, and operational excellence across all AAIG member airlines.

Fastjet MAX

Fastjet MAX is AAIG’s dedicated low‑cost carrier, designed to make air travel accessible across Africa. With efficient operations, fast turnarounds, and routes connecting underserved cities, Fastjet MAX empowers mobility, opportunity, and economic growth across the continent.

Arabic Airways

Arabic Airways connects the Middle East and Africa with premium service, modern aircraft, and luxury‑focused cabins. Its Gulf‑based hubs provide strategic access to Asia, Europe, and beyond. Arabic Airways strengthens AAIG’s global reach and brings world‑class hospitality to the alliance.

FlyAmerican

FlyAmerican expands AAIG’s presence across North America, offering strong domestic coverage and seamless transatlantic connections
